FAQs

Got Questions?
We've Got Answers.

What are your opening hours?+

Open every day including weekends and public holidays — 10:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Last entry at 9:00 PM.

What ages are the games for?+

We have activities for kids aged 2 to 13. Soft Play for toddlers (2–5), Trampoline, Cricket and Arcade for older kids (6–13).

Do parents need to pay entry?+

Parents get free entry to the café and waiting area. Parents who wish to participate in activities can buy individual tokens at the counter.

How do I book the Cricket Bowling Machine?+

Book online via our website using the "Book Now" button. Slots start from ₹100 for 15 minutes. Walk-ins also welcome based on availability.

How do I plan a birthday party?+

Call us on +91 98765 43210. We'll customise a package for your guest count, theme and budget. Minimum 3 days advance booking needed.

Is the facility safe and clean?+

All equipment and play areas are sanitised multiple times daily. Staff are first-aid trained. Fully air-conditioned with CCTV monitoring throughout.

Can we bring outside food?+

Outside food is not permitted inside the play area. Our café serves freshly prepared food and beverages for kids and adults at reasonable prices.

What is your cancellation policy?+

Cricket slot cancellations 4+ hours in advance receive a full refund. Birthday party deposits are non-refundable but transferable to a new date.

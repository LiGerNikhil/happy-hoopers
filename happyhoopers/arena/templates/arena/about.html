More Than Just a Play Arena

Happy Hoopers was born from a simple dream: to create a safe, fun, and exciting space where children can be children - running, jumping, playing, and making memories that last a lifetime.

Founded in 2023, we started with a vision to bring world-class entertainment facilities to Vijayawada. Today, we're proud to be the city's most loved kids' play arena, serving hundreds of families every week.

Our state-of-the-art facility combines the latest in play equipment with traditional values of safety, hygiene, and family fun. Every detail is designed with your child's happiness and safety in mind.

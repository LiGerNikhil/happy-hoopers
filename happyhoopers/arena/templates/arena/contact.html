Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions
Quick Answers!

Do I need to book in advance?

Walk-ins are welcome, but we recommend booking during weekends and holidays to avoid disappointment. For birthday parties, advance booking is required.

What age groups do you cater to?

Our play arena is designed for children aged 2-13 years. We have different zones suitable for various age groups within this range.

Is there parking available?

Yes, we have ample parking space available for both two-wheelers and four-wheelers at no additional cost.

Can parents stay while kids play?

Absolutely! Parents are welcome to stay and supervise. We have a comfortable café area where you can relax while keeping an eye on your kids.

Do you have party packages?

Yes! We offer various birthday party packages starting from ₹4,999. Each package includes exclusive arena access, food, and decorations.

Is the facility safe and hygienic?

Safety and hygiene are our top priorities. All equipment is regularly sanitized, and we have trained staff supervising the play areas at all times.
